About Our Team

The Robotic Raiders are a dedicated group of students from Delview Secondary School in Delta, British Columbia. Founded in October 2022, our team has grown from complete beginners to competitive FTC participants.

We believe in teamwork, continuous learning, and gracious professionalism. Each team member contributes their unique skills and perspectives to make our team successful.
