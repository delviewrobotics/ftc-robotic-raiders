Awards

Recognizing our team's achievements and contributions

CONNECT AWARD

2024/2025

During our qualifiers at L.A. Matheson, our team won the Connect Award. The Connect Award is presented to the team which demonstrates the most connection with their local science, technology, engineering, and math (STEM) community. Our team was recognized for our outstanding aid in helping our community understand FIRST, the FIRST Tech Challenge, and the team itself.
