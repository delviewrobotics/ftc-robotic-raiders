Outreach

Our commitment to inspiring the next generation of STEM leaders

At Robotic Raiders, we believe in giving back to our community. We actively participate in outreach events to share our passion for robotics and STEM education with others. Our team members volunteer their time to inspire younger students and demonstrate what's possible through robotics.

Our Events

Wesbrooke Community Centre

Our team visited the Wesbrooke Community Centre to demonstrate our robot and share our FTC experience with community members. We showcased our robot's capabilities and talked about the engineering process.

Wesbrooke Community Centre

Team members engaging with visitors and explaining the basics of robotics and programming. We encouraged young visitors to consider joining robotics programs.

STEMastery Workshop

We participated in STEMastery workshops to help introduce younger students to the world of robotics and engineering. These hands-on sessions let kids experience building and programming first-hand.

Virtual Mentorship Session

Our team connected with mentors virtually to learn about careers in STEM and gain valuable insights from industry professionals.

Awards & Recognition

Connect Award

We are proud to have received the Connect Award, which recognizes teams that demonstrate exceptional community outreach and engagement. This award reflects our team's commitment to spreading STEM education beyond our school.
