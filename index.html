Meet Our Team

About Us

We are an FTC school team based in Delview Secondary in Delta, British Columbia; our team was created in October 2022. We are all robot enthusiasts and enjoy spending time working together to strive toward the common goal of becoming a competitive robotics team in the region.

This is our fourth time competing in the FIRST Tech Challenge. Although many of our members lack proper experience in working with robots, we are quickly learning and adapting to the different strategies and tactics involved in FTC.

Learn More

2024-25 Season: INTO THE DEEP

As we enter our fourth season in FTC, our team aims to take a step towards our common goal of becoming a more competitive team in the region.

We aim to complete this through simultaneously maintaining gracious professionalism by encouraging high-quality work and emphasizing respect for the community. Despite many of our members having no experience in FTC, we are all learning and getting more actively involved.
