Our Robot

History of our robot designs through the seasons

2024-25 Season: INTO THE DEEP

Robot Specifications

  • Drive System: Mecanum Wheels
  • Control System: REV Control Hub
  • Frame: REV/goBILDA Channel
  • Lift System: Linear Slides with String/Pulley
  • Intake: Dual-Wheel Mechanism

Key Features

Our INTO THE DEEP robot is designed to collect and score samples efficiently. The mecanum drive system allows for omnidirectional movement, while our linear slide system provides the reach needed for high-level scoring.

Robot History

2023-24: CENTERSTAGE

Our third season robot featured improvements in reliability and scoring mechanisms. We focused on consistent pixel placement and efficient autonomous routines.

2022-23: POWERPLAY

Our inaugural season! As a new team, we learned the fundamentals of robot building and FTC competition. This robot taught us valuable lessons about design iteration and teamwork.

Technical Documentation

Chassis Design

Our robot uses a combination of REV and goBILDA components for a sturdy yet lightweight frame. The mecanum wheel configuration allows for omnidirectional movement.

Programming

We use Java with Android Studio for our robot programming. Our code includes TeleOp controls, autonomous routines, and sensor integration for competition.

Mechanisms

Our manipulator systems are designed for the specific game challenges. We use a combination of servos, motors, and custom 3D printed parts.
